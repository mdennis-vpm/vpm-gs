"Rotate" animation, enabled by adding the class .vpa-rotate-(direction)-(degrees) to an element. There are 6 total options:

  • .vpa-rotate-cw-90
  • .vpa-rotate-cw-180
  • .vpa-rotate-cw-360
  • .vpa-rotate-ccw-90
  • .vpa-rotate-ccw-180
  • .vpa-rotate-ccw-360

cw = clockwise
ccw = counter-clockwise

Element will begin rotated by the given amount in the opposite direction given.
When triggered, the element will rotate into place, in the given direction.

(In tandem with .vpa-out class, element will instead rotate OUT of orientation, in the opposite direction given.)
