"Slide In From Right" animation, enabled by adding the class .gs-slide-in-from-right to an element.

Element will begin pushed off to the right of the viewport. When triggered, the element will slide into view and into it's designed place. By default, animation eases out.

Note: recommended to add overflow-x: hidden; to body, to avoid horizontal scrollbar appearing.
