.vpa-out

"Out/To" modifier, enabled by adding the class .vpa-out to an element.

By default, animations animate INTO place, FROM the modified properties of the animations.
This class will alter the animations to animate OUT of their designed positions/properties, TO the modified properties of the animations.

For example:
.vpa-fade instead of fading IN, will fade OUT.
.vpa-slide-top instead of sliding down FROM the top, will slide up TO the top.
