.vpa-on-scroll-to
.vpa-slide-top

 

"On-scroll-to" animation trigger, enabled by adding the class .vpa-on-scroll-to to an element.

Sets the animation to trigger and play when the element is scrolled far enough into view.
Trigger looks at element's designed position, rather than modified positioning via animation properties.

(The .vpa-out class has no impact on this property)
